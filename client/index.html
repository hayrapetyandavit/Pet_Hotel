Vaccination requirements

We care about the health of all our furry guests is, that's why not vaccinated pets are not allowed in our hotel.Vaccinations must be administered at least 48 hours prior to arrival under the guidance of a licensed veterinarian. However, we prefer vaccinations be administered 10-14 days before check-in to ensure maximum efficiency. Written proof is required. You must also show proof of your pet meeting state and local pet boarding vaccination requirements.

Cancellation and refund

We are working hard to provide best services to our guests and we are preparing rooms and playing spaces, food, etc. after your booking is confirmed. So please be sure that you warned us beforehand if your arrival is going to delay or cancel. Refund policy is:
More than 24 hours before check-in date: FREE CANCELLATION
0-24 hours before check-in date: 1 night cost will be charged as cancellation fee
In case of no show: Entire booking cost will be charged as cancellation fee
